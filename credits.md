# Credits

## Data sources
This project uses publicly available datasets. Unless otherwise noted, data were downloaded from "Our World in Data" (Grapher) and include the processing described in each dataset's metadata. 

- **AI training compute vs. parameters (by researcher affiliation)** - Epoch AI (2025), with major processing by Our World in Data. (File: `ai-training-computation-vs-parameters-by-researcher-affiliation.csv`)
- **Child mortality rate** - Gapminder (2015); UN Inter-agency Group for Child Mortality Estimation (2025) - processed by Our World in Data. (Files: `child-mortality.csv`, `child-mortality.metadata.json`)
- **Per capita primary energy consumption by source** - Energy Institute - Statistical Review of World Energy (2025); Population based on various sources (2024) - with major processing by Our World in Data. (Files: `per-capita-energy-stacked.csv`, `per-capita-energy-stacked.metadata.json`)
- **Per capita meat and fish consumption** - Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (2024) - processed by Our World in Data. (Files: `per-capita-meat-type.csv`, `per-capita-meat-type.metadata.json`)
- **Population by age group** - UN, World Population Prospects (2024) - processed by Our World in Data. (Files: `population-by-age-group-with-projections.csv`, `population-by-age-group-with-projections.metadata.json`)
- **Income inequality: Gini coefficient** - World Bank Poverty and Inequality Platform (2025) - with major processing by Our World in Data. (Files: `economic-inequality-gini-index.csv`, `economic-inequality-gini-index.metadata.json`)
- **Share of the population using the Internet** - World Telecommunication/ICT Indicators Database - International Telecommunication Union (ITU), via World Bank (2026) - with minor processing by Our World in Data. (Files: `share-of-individuals-using-the-internet.csv`, `share-of-individuals-using-the-internet.metadata.json`)
